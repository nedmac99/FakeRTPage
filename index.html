About us

Rooster Teeth is a pioneering online entertainment company founded in 2003, known for its diverse content in gaming, animation, comedy, and web series. Initially rising to fame with "Red vs. Blue," a popular machinima series created from the game "Halo," the company expanded its offerings to include hits like "RWBY," "Achievement Hunter," and "Camp Camp." Through platforms like YouTube and its subscription service FIRST, Rooster Teeth engages millions of fans with exclusive content and early access. Acquired by Fullscreen in 2014 and subsequently becoming part of WarnerMedia, this partnership facilitated growth, enabling Rooster Teeth to maintain its innovative storytelling and community engagement. Renowned for its adaptability in digital media and hosting events like RTX, Rooster Teeth continues to be a significant force in online entertainment, connecting with audiences worldwide.
